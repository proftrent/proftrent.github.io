How I work

I approach learning products as systems: learner needs, content architecture, technology, operations, and outcomes all have to work together.

My role is often to connect the pieces.

I bridge pedagogy, technology, and product strategy—helping teams evaluate emerging tools, define learning architectures, create scalable workflows, and build experiences that feel practical for learners and manageable for organizations.

Define the learning architecture.
Clarify audience, outcomes, progression, assessment strategy, and the learner journey.
Translate strategy into products.
Design digital experiences, activities, media, tools, and support systems that can scale.
Prototype, test, and improve.
Use feedback, analytics, pilots, and stakeholder input to refine the learner experience.
Operationalize quality.
Create standards, workflows, templates, and tools that help teams produce consistent work.
